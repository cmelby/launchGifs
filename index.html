Our Story

PHASE Watches was created by a couple of young individuals who are passionate about watches and were frustrated with traditional watches’ lack of customization and high price point. 

We’re inspired by, and enjoy wearing high-quality watches but at a reasonable cost. We also found that wearing the same watch day in and day out felt boring and wanted interchangeable bands that the apple watch has but for a traditional watch. 

Generally, an interactable band adds cost where there doesn’t need to be. The watch industry is filled with high retail markups for low-quality watches, we set out to find a solution. 

So over the past year and a half, we’ve built relationships with leaders in the watch industry to refine our exciting collection, custom-made and curated specifically for you. With our stylish watch collection, we focused on three core details: 

  • The highest quality materials producing a very stylish and minimal design
  • Giving you the power to further design your watch with interchangeable bands
  • Offering high-quality timepieces at an affordable cost

With PHASE Watches, the power is yours to choose what adventure you want with our diverse selection of luxury watches. We simply want to be a guide to help inspire and empower you with this choice.

Never settle. A new PHASE of stylish, high-quality, affordable watches is here. A timeless selection that we know you will enjoy for a lifetime.
